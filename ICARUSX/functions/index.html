Initiative

With your help we can grow a charitable account to make annualized donations to schools and to communities in need.

Introduction

In a world where the rich will continue to get richer, Icarus Investments feels morally obligated to manage and create an investment account for under-privileged and poorer communities. Our intentions are to donate profits on an annual basis to charities and schools that need it the most. Over the years, poorly funded schools have had to make budget cuts that cut out important curriculums. Quality education should not be about money and should be focused on the students. We hope to revitalize Arts and Music curriculums to bolster creativity, provide quality education resources and technology and create a vetting program for exceptional and inspiring teachers over the coming years. We believe that progress can be made with time and initiative from one generation to the next. If you believe that children in poorer communities deserve a better shot at success or helping to minimize our massive wealth gap, please support our project!
